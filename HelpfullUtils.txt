

Regular Expressions for PHP Storm to escape twig tags
Needed if you build new templates
=======================================================

search:
(\{\{ [ a-zA-Z()_\'\.\:\"|\-\,]* \}\})
(\{\% [ a-zA-Z()_\'\.\:\"|\-\,\/\{\}]* \%\})
(\{\%- [ a-zA-Z()_\'\.\:\"|\-\,\{\}]* -\%\})
(\{\%- [ a-zA-Z()_\'\.\:\"|\-\,\{\}]* \%\})
(\{\% [ a-zA-Z()_\'\.\:\"|\-\,\{\}]* -\%\})

replace:
\{\{ \'$1\' \}\}
